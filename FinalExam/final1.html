What is Photography?

Photography is the art of capturing light to create images that tell stories, preserve moments, and ignite emotion. From sweeping landscapes to candid portraits, photography transforms the ordinary into the extraordinary by freezing time in a single frame.
Whether you're using a smartphone, DSLR, or vintage film camera, the process combines creativity, technique, and vision to produce images that resonate. This skill while always artistic in nature, can be applied to a variety of types of work including commercial applications for individuals and businesses alike, as well as more artistic applications that are meant to spark a visceral reaction.
On this site, you’ll explore multiple facets of photography, including types of cameras, photography mediums, as well as multiple applications of photography. Whether you're a budding photographer or a curious observer, there’s something here for every eye behind the lens.
